About Me

I am a Data Engineer with over 5 years of experience in building and maintaining end-to-end ETL pipelines across cloud and on-premise environments. I specialize in designing scalable, reliable, and high-performance data workflows for both batch and streaming data using tools like Apache Airflow, Azure Data Factory, and Google Cloud Dataflow.

My core expertise includes Python programming (Pandas, NumPy), SQL, data modeling, and cloud data warehouses like BigQuery and Snowflake. I also bring strong experience in query optimization for both SQL and NoSQL databases such as MySQL, MongoDB, and Cosmos DB.

I'm passionate about building high-quality data solutions that are robust, maintainable, and transparent. I have worked closely with analysts, data scientists, and business stakeholders to deliver model-ready datasets and data products that support advanced analytics and AI/ML initiatives.

I thrive in collaborative environments and am committed to continuous improvement and clear documentation. With a keen eye for data quality, I implement validations, exception handling, and automated monitoring across data pipelines.
